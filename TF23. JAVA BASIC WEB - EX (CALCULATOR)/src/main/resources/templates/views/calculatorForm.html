Calculator functions:

  • '+' plus - sum Left and Right Operand;
  • '-' minus - subtracts Left Operand from Right Operand;
  • '*' multiplication - multiplies Left and Right Operand;
  • '/' division - divide Left and Right Operand;
  • '^' power - power Left Operand with Right Operand;
  • 'mod' modulo - modulo Left Operand with Right Operand;
  • '√' square root - square root of Left Operand (Right Operand - readonly);
  • 'sin' sin function - sin function of Left Operand in DEG (Right Operand - readonly);
  • 'cos' cos function - cos function of Left Operand in DEG (Right Operand - readonly);
  • 'tan' tan function - tan function of Left Operand in DEG (Right Operand - readonly);
  • '!' factorial - factorial of Left Operand (Right Operand - readonly);
  • 'ln' natural logarithm - natural logarithm of Left Operand (Right Operand - readonly);
